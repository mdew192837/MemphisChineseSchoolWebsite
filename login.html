The $10 registration fee will be waived if tuition is paid in full by August 21, 2016. Our administrators work hard during the registration period. Please do your part to minimize their work load.

Refund Policy:

For regular 12 to 15 session classes, students can get full refund after the first session, 75% after the 2nd session, 50% after the 3rd session and no refund after the 4th session. For classes with fewer sessions, only 1 free trial and 50% for the second trial. You need to notify the school admin before Wednesday following your trial class. Changing classes is considered dropping one class and adding another one. Changing from one Chinese class to another Chinese class is not considered class change.
